What is this?

This page demonstrates a full-screen hero with a real-time, shader-based sine wave animation rendered using Three.js. The canvas is fully responsive, high-DPI aware, and runs efficiently with requestAnimationFrame.

Smooth Animation

GPU-accelerated vertex displacement using custom shaders for fluid motion.

Responsive

Automatically resizes to fit the viewport and device pixel ratio.

Accessible

Overlay content remains readable with color contrast and reduced motion support.

Get Started

  1. Use the hero as a background for your landing page.
  2. Adjust colors, speeds, and amplitudes in the shader uniforms.
  3. Add your own content above the animated canvas.
